Data Engineers

Solita

Location Job type
Gothenburg, Sweden Full-time
Salary Apply latest by
Paid 22.5.2019
Job Description
As a Data Engineer you will build technical solutions that consist of data models, data integration, storage and cloud based data pipeline. You will work 90-95% hands-on with configuration, integration, installation, scripting and 5-10% with designing architectures and explaining the solution to customers and team members. You will sit at the same table with the top leadership of our customers, IT and business decision-makers and data experts and use your skills and vision to support the development of our customers’ business.
Requirement
Have experience and interest in cloud technologies (especially in AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
Have experience in one or more of the following: data modelling, data warehousing, databases (SQL/NoSQL) and ETL/ELT. Experience in data warehouse automation in considered as a plus.
Can build data pipelines in one of the following programming languages: Python, R or Java.
Are familiar with the software development practices such as version control and CI/CD.

Algorithm Test Specialist

Oura

Location Job type Salary Apply latest by
Oulu, Finland Full-time Paid 09.30.2019
Job Description

As an Algorithm Test Specialist, your main responsibility will be to test and verify that our algorithms will work as intended. We would appreciate if you are also able to develop test automation to improve the verification process related to algorithm testing. Most of our algorithms are developed using Matlab and then implemented into our product as embedded software. That’s why we expect you to be familiar with Matlab and C-programming. You will work as a part of our Testing team with close co-operation with our Science and Firmware teams...

Requirement
  • Have at least 3+ years of experience in similar roles.
  • Are fluent in Matlab and C-programming.
  • Have experience in software development processes.
  • Have an interest in human physiology.
  • Are a self-driven worker
  • Have good problem-solving skills and an analytical mindset.
  • Have a good resilience to stress under pressure.
  • Have good written and spoken English skills.
  • Preferably some familiarity with unit and functional testing (Jest, Cypress, PHPUnit).
