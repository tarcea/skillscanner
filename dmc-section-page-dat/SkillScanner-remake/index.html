About us

SkillScanner is a network with a diverse range of English speaking professionals, from an array of cultural and professional backgrounds, connected together through the SkillScanner platform. SkillScanner is the first service that streamlines the job hunting process for international talents within the Nordics. SkillScanner offers personalised, affordable services for companies looking to hire from our niche market of English Speaking professionals.
